<div class="section-label">Behaviour</div>
<p>Wooly Moss Moose forage for Rolling Moss ("Roly Polys") and swallow them whole like jawbreakers. They hold them in their mouths, slowly licking away all the moss until only the bare rock core remains, then spit out the stones. Trails of spit-slick rocks are a reliable sign of Moose grazing activity.</p>
<p>When threatened, they spit half-licked moss balls at enemies with surprising force and accuracy.</p>
