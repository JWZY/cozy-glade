<p>A giant owl with a single large eye centered on its face, ecologically similar to a golden eagle or great horned owl. Its feathers shimmer with an unusual quality that allows it to blend seamlessly into the night sky, becoming nearly invisible against the stars.</p>

<p>The Night Strix can trap creatures in another dimension, presumably a pocket realm accessible only through its cyclopean gaze.
