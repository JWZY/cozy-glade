<div class="bestiary-content">
<div class="bestiary-section">
<div class="section-label">Description</div>
<p>A peculiar creature resembling a wallaby with an oversized, bushy squirrel tail and elegant deer-like antlers. Their soft fur and large eyes give them an endearing appearance. Named for the distinctive chittering "jub-jub" sounds they make when communicating.</p>
</div>

<div class="bestiary-section">
<div class="section-label">Behavior</div>
<p>Jub-jubs are social creatures that travel in small packs. When threatened, they can shift into an ethereal form, becoming intangible and nearly invisible to avoid detection. They obsessively collect teeth, which they store carefully in their marsupial pouches—a defense mechanism to ward off the Night Strix, which has a strong aversion to creatures carrying teeth.</p>
</div>

<div class="bestiary-section">
<div class="section-label">Field Notes</div>
<p>Remarkably, Jub-jubs are willing to trade for teeth—offering unknown goods or services in exchange. The purpose of their tooth collection remains a mystery. Approach slowly and consider bringing dental offerings if you wish to interact.</p>
</div>
